About Me

I'm a Full Stack Web, Mobile, and AI Developer based in Kyiv, Ukraine. I love building applications that solve real-world problems and are delightful to use. My specialities include React, Next.js, React Native, TypeScript, and integrating AI features into both web and mobile apps.

This role taught me strong presentation and communication skills, effective collaboration in an international environment, and how to deliver high-quality work under tight deadlines and high-pressure conditions.
